## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Activities (15 mins/day)

1. **Respond** to all comments within 2 hours
2. **Engage** with 10 target accounts' posts
3. **Comment** thoughtfully on industry content
4. **Share** relevant third-party content (1x/day)

### Weekly Activities

1. **DM** new connection requests with personalized message
2. **Join** 2-3 relevant LinkedIn/Twitter conversations
3. **Repurpose** top-performing content
4. **Analyze** metrics and adjust

## Platform-Specific Best Practices

### LinkedIn

- Post between 8-10 AM or 12-1 PM (local time)
- Use line breaks for readability
- First line is crucial (hook)
- End with question or CTA
- Respond to comments immediately (algorithm boost)

### Twitter/X

- Post 4-6 times per day (including replies)
- Thread format for detailed content
- Quote tweet instead of retweet
- Engage in replies before posting
- Use visuals when possible

### Instagram

- Post at 11 AM or 7-8 PM
- First 3 seconds of video = hook
- Use all carousel slides (engagement)
- Stories: 5-7 per day
- Reels: 2-3 per week
