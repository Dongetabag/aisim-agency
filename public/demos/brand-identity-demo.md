## Typography

### Primary Font: Inter

**Why Inter:** Modern, highly legible, excellent for both digital and print. Free and widely supported.

| Style | Weight | Size | Usage |
|-------|--------|------|-------|
| H1 | Bold (700) | 48-72px | Hero headlines |
| H2 | Bold (700) | 36-48px | Section headers |
| H3 | Semibold (600) | 24-32px | Sub-sections |
| H4 | Semibold (600) | 18-24px | Card titles |
| Body | Regular (400) | 16-18px | Paragraph text |
| Small | Regular (400) | 14px | Captions, labels |
| Button | Semibold (600) | 16px | CTAs, buttons |

## Brand Voice

### Personality Traits

1. **Innovative** - We're at the cutting edge of AI technology
2. **Confident** - We know our solutions work
3. **Clear** - No jargon, no fluff, just results
4. **Approachable** - Technology made accessible
5. **Results-Focused** - Everything ties back to outcomes

### Tone Guidelines

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| "Transform your business with AI" | "Leverage synergistic AI paradigms" |
| "Save 40 hours per week" | "Optimize temporal resource allocation" |
| "Here's how it works" | "Let us elucidate the methodology" |
| "Real results from real businesses" | "Unprecedented ROI trajectories" |

### Writing Examples

**Headline:** "Stop Losing Leads. Start Closing Deals."
**Subhead:** "AI automation that works while you sleep."
**CTA:** "Book Your Strategy Call"
**Value Prop:** "Deploy AI sales agents in 48 hours. No code required."

## Imagery Style

### Photography

- **Style:** Clean, modern, tech-forward
- **Subjects:** Business professionals, technology, workspaces
- **Mood:** Confident, innovative, successful
- **Treatment:** High contrast, slight blue/purple tint

### Illustrations

- **Style:** Modern line art with gradient fills
- **Elements:** Abstract tech patterns, circuit-like designs
- **Colors:** Brand palette with gradient overlays

### Icons

- **Style:** Lucide React icon set (or similar)
- **Weight:** Regular (1.5px stroke)
- **Size:** 24px standard, scale proportionally

### Patterns

**Tech Grid Pattern:**
```
A subtle grid pattern used in backgrounds, representing data and connectivity.
Color: rgba(255,255,255,0.03) on dark backgrounds
```
